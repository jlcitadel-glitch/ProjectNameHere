# Knight/Guard - 10-Step Design Card

## Step 1 - Combat Role: Tank
Frontline blocker. Holds ground, absorbs attention, punishes careless aggression.
Patience test, not a damage race.

## Step 2 - Behavior Axes
- Aggression: 2/5 (reactive, not proactive)
- Mobility: 2/5 (slow patrol, moderate chase)
- Range: 1/5 (melee only, longsword sweep)
- Predictability: 4/5 (highly telegraphed, consistent)

## Step 3 - Player Verb Tested: Parry + Spacing
Primary: parry (ideal training dummy with slow telegraphed swings).
Secondary: spacing (bait attack, step back, punish recovery).

## Step 4 - Threat Clock: Delayed
Spawns patrolling. ~1.5s alert delay before chase. Moderate chase speed.
Dangerous through persistence, not speed.

## Step 5 - Movement Identity: Patrol -> Chase (Anchor)
- Patrol: slow back-and-forth, turns at walls/ledges
- Chase: walks toward player (slower than player run), doesn't jump
- Anchor: returns to patrol zone if player kites too far (leash)

## Step 6 - Encounter Synergies
- Nightmare: Knight + Bat(s) - ground lock + aerial harassment
- Training: Knight + Slime(s) - obvious priority target
- Future: Knight + Mage - tank + artillery classic comp

## Step 7 - Commitment Profile: Pattern-locked
- Wind-up: 0.4s telegraph
- Active: 0.3s sweep (parry window)
- Recovery: 0.6s vulnerable (exploit window)
- Parry stagger: 1.0s
- Attack cooldown: 1.2s
- No feints - always follows through

## Step 8 - Counterplay Map
- Parry: Excellent (staggers 1.0s, reflect damage)
- Dodge-through: Good (dash through sweep, punish recovery)
- Jump-over: Good (no anti-air)
- Spacing/kiting: Partial (works but slow, high HP)
- Ranged: Partial (safe but many hits needed)
- Trading hits: Poor (high HP, loses trade)
- Rushing: Fails (contact damage + sweep catches)

## Step 9 - Death Consequence: Area Clear
High XP drop. No arena effect. Territory becomes safe.
Higher health orb drop chance as "well fought" reward.

## Step 10 - Density Scaling
- 1x: Core experience, solo parry trainer
- 2x: Overlapping threat zones, staggered timings
- 3x: Wall of knights, use sparingly (late waves)
- 5x+: Not viable pure, becomes boring HP wall
- Mixed: 1-2 Knights + faster enemies is the sweet spot
- Scale HP more than damage (durability role, not burst)
